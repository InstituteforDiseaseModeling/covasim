==================
Welcome to |Cov_s|
==================

|Cov_l| is a stochastic agent-based simulator designed to be used for COVID-19
(novel coronavirus, SARS-CoV-2) epidemic analyses. These include projections of
indicators such as numbers of infections and peak hospital demand. |Cov_s| can
also be used to explore the potential impact of different interventions.
